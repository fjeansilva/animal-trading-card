White Tiger

White tigers are found in many major zoos across India. Nandankanan Zoological Park, Odisha hosts 34 white tigers with the first litter of white tigers born in 1980.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Weight: 200 to 230 kilograms
  • Grow up: 3 meters in length
  • Habitat: Steamy hot jungles and icy cold forest

At the beginning of this century it is estimated that there were 100,000 wild tigers, today the number is less than 8,000. Simply put, tigers are disappearing in the wild. The main threats to tigers are poaching, habitat loss and population fragmentation.
